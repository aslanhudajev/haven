# More Features Todo

Use this file as the running backlog for larger product features to build and ship one by one.

## Stuff We Can Build Ourselves

- Income-based household split
Salary input, fairness logic, and category-based contribution ratios like `60/40` for groceries.
- Household reconciliation
Month-end review of who paid what, what each person should have paid, and final settlement calculation.
- Household-first dashboard
Default shared view focused on `our spending`, not `my spending`.
- Category budgeting
Pre-filled categories, per-category budgets, and budget tracking.
- Recurring household costs
Rent, utilities, subscriptions, and other fixed costs that repeat every period.
- Budget alerts and notifications
Warnings like `90% of food budget used`.
- Shared goals and projects
Long-term shared savings goals and quick temporary budgets or projects.
- Shared shopping list with budget logging
Item planning, estimated prices, and auto-logging into the budget when completed.
- History and insights
Trend comparisons, spending summaries, and simple household analytics.
- Chores tracker
Shared household chores, assignments, completion tracking, and fairness visibility.
- Shared household calendar
Shared calendar for bills, chores, due dates, events, shopping plans, and project milestones.
- Household subscription model
One subscription per household instead of per person.
- Free vs premium product structure
Decide which features are free and which are premium inside the app.

If it needs to feel automatic, accurate, and emotionally low-friction at month end.

- Shared goals and projects
If we want goals, temporary budgets, and household budget to coexist cleanly.
